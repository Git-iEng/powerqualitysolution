Power Quality Solutions & Electrical Reliability

Power quality solutions address variations in voltage and current that impact the reliability of electrical systems. Issues arise when the power supplied doesn‚Äôt match what sensitive loads require, often due to the growing use of power electronics and other non-linear loads, which can introduce harmonics and reduce overall distribution efficiency.

The consequences of poor power quality can be significant equipment overheating, overloading of neutral conductors, skin effect losses, nuisance tripping of circuit breakers, and general performance degradation. Improving power quality is essential for stable, efficient operations.

We design, manufacture, supply, and commission a complete range of power quality products and associated equipment to ensure clean, stable power supporting uptime, efficiency, and asset longevity.

Active Harmonic Filter (AHF)

Purpose: Reduce harmonic distortion from nonlinear loads (VFDs, LED lighting, UPS, etc.).

Benefits:

  • Real-time harmonic compensation.
  • Handles multiple harmonics up to the 50th order.
  • Reduces energy loss due to harmonic currents.
  • Supports compliance with IEEE and IEC standards.

Power Factor Correction Panels (PFC)

Purpose: Compensate reactive power to improve power factor and system efficiency.

Benefits:

  • Minimizes reactive power.
  • Reduces electricity bills by improving the power factor.
  • Improves overall system efficiency.
  • Manual or automatic control options.

Voltage Stabilisation & Sag Correction (Stabilizer + DVR)

Purpose: Stabilize voltage and rapidly correct sags/momentary interruptions to protect critical loads.

Benefits:

  • Regulates overvoltage and undervoltage conditions.
  • Instantly restores voltage during sags or brief outages.
  • Maintains continuity of operations in critical systems.
  • Protects motors, compressors, and sensitive electronics.

Surge Protection Panels

Purpose: Protect electrical systems from transient overvoltages due to lightning, switching, or disturbances.

Benefits:

  • Shields against short-term voltage spikes.
  • Enhances the lifespan of electrical equipment.
  • Improves system reliability.

Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)

Purpose: Provide backup power in case of grid failure or power disruptions.

Benefits:

  • Prevents downtime during power outages.
  • Protects against voltage surges, sags, and transients.
  • Ensures data integrity and equipment safety.

Load Bank Panels

Purpose: Simulate full load on an electrical system or generator to verify performance.

Benefits:

  • Verifies that backup systems can handle full load.
  • Ensures reliable operation of generators and UPS systems.
  • Used for commissioning and maintenance of electrical systems.

Energy Management System (EMS) Panels

Purpose: Monitor, control, and optimize energy consumption across different systems.

Benefits:

  • Provides real-time monitoring of energy consumption.
  • Identifies areas for energy saving and optimization.
  • Enhances energy efficiency and reduces utility costs.

Frequency Converter Panels

Purpose: Convert supply frequency (e.g., 50 Hz to 60 Hz) to match equipment requirements.

Benefits:

  • Ensures equipment operates at the correct frequency.
  • Protects against frequency-related damage.
  • Enhances the efficiency of frequency-sensitive equipment.

Integrated Power Quality Panel

Purpose: A comprehensive solution integrating multiple power quality devices into one panel.

Benefits:

  • Combines several technologies into one coordinated panel.
  • Provides holistic power quality management.
  • Reduces the risk of equipment failure and nuisance trips.

Our Process

Power Quality Project Workflow

A standards-driven workflow covering PQ audits, design & simulation, manufacturing, installation and commissioning of PFC, RTPFC, Active/Passive/Hybrid Filters, and sine-wave/dv/dt solutions.

View Projects

  1. Assessment & PQ Audit

    Site survey, load profiling, data logging; THD/voltage flicker/imbalance checks; PFC requirement study; compliance gap to IEEE 519/IEC standards; risk & savings report.

  2. Design & Simulation

    Filter topology selection (Active/Passive/Hybrid, detuned/tuned); kvar/Amp sizing; NEPLAN-based studies; protection coordination; panel GA & BOM; performance guarantees vs targets.

  3. Manufacturing & FAT

    Panel fabrication (APFC/RTPFC/AHF/Hybrid/Sine-wave/dv/dt); wiring & functional tests; controller programming; FAT with test reports; logistics & site readiness checklist.

  4. Installation, Commissioning & Handover

    Mounting, cabling, earthing; protection & interlocks; tuning & baseline THD/PF verification; SCADA/PQ meter integration; O&M training; final compliance report & after-sales support plan.
